Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ's)

What should I bring to my pet's appointment?

Bring any previous medical records, a list of medications, and if possible, a stool or urine sample for routine testing. If your pet is anxious, bring a favourite toy or blanket.

Do you offer payment plans for expensive treatments?

Yes — for major surgeries and long-term treatments we can discuss payment options. Speak to our Office Manager for details.

Can I request a home visit?

We offer limited home visits for elderly or immobile patients. Please call to check availability and fees.
